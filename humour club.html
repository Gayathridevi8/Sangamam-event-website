ABOUT US

We conduct events to make people laugh.

The Humour Club of KPRIET enhances young minds to tap into fun-loving side. The main motive of this club is to “put everyone at ease” and to make everyone afloat with amusements and laugh. It gives them a chance to use the showcase platform where they can expose their skills like standup comedy, mimicry,spoof,performing funny skills etc. The mission of humour club is to introduce new talents to the media and also to the cinemas by using their talents.

"Humour is by far the most significant activity of the human brain"

- Edward de Bora
