ABOUT US

We spread awareness on importance of nature and the need to protect it.

The Eco Club of KPRIET is committed towards raising social awareness about the environmental issues. It encourages students to participate actively in the club's activities. The mission of this club is to implement a Plastic Free Campus and to increase awareness among the public through volunteers.

"To forget how to dig the earth and to tend the soil is to forget ourselves"

- Mahatma Gandhi
