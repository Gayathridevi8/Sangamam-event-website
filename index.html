sangamam -An Echoing Symphony

An Embodiment of 17 Clubs

Sangamam , the Unity of arts is an enabler of all the initiatives. The Team Sangamam is a manifestation of 17 clubs to bring out the real potential of our students and to enrich their skills. During the Covid-19 pandemic situation, KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology inaugurated the grand Sangamam for conducting online webinars and various types of cultural and educative events for school and college students. E-certificates are provided for both the internal and external participants as a token of appreciation.
