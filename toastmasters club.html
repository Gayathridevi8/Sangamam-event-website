ABOUT US

We help students overcome stage fear and speak fluently

Toastmasters KPRIET is a club that provides suitable environment for the students who strive to achieve eloquence in public speaking, enhancing their communication skills, leadership skills and mastering stage skills. We enrich our skills by doing many fun things which will help us achieve our vision. which includes conducting many exciting contests and events as well as informative webinars. The goal of toastmasters club is to provide maximum support to the college in terms of MOC/Anchoring, engage freshers and enhance their team building and leadership skills and to provide opportunities for all the students to board the stage. The club will continue the endeavour to escalate the talents and help others to explore their inner self by creating a stimulating environment.
